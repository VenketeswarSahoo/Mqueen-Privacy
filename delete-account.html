How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account from the Mqueen task management application, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the Mqueen mobile application on your device.
  2. Go to the Settings menu.
  3. Scroll down and find the Delete My Account option.
  4. Tap on Delete My Account.
  5. A confirmation message will appear asking if you are sure about this action.
  6. Confirm the deletion by tapping Yes, Delete My Account.
  7. Your account will be permanently deleted, and all your data will be erased.

Important Notes:

  • Once deleted, your account cannot be restored.
  • Ensure you have backed up any important data before proceeding.
  • If you need assistance, contact our support team before deleting your account.

We are sorry to see you go. If you change your mind, you can always create a new account.
